During my internship at ZionLab IT Consultancy, I had the opportunity to work on a web-based POS management system. To build this system, I utilized a combination of React.js and Next.js frameworks, along with a free template that I selected to save time. However, the template didn't have all the required features for the project, so I had to add them by studying and implementing them myself. The experience of working on this project was challenging, but it helped me gain valuable knowledge and practical experience in software development. It also helped me to further develop my problem-solving skills, and I gained a deeper understanding of how to work with a team of developers to accomplish project goals. Overall, this project was a rewarding experience that allowed me to enhance my technical and interpersonal skills.
